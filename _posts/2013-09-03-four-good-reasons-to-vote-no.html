--- layout: post title: Four good reasons to vote “No” on Nov. 5 published: true ---
  1. Jackson County should not be up to its eyeballs in the medical research business.
  2. The proponents’ contention that the tax proceeds will significantly boost “economic development” is based strictly on trickle-down economics.
  3. This venture should be privately funded, at least for the most part.
  4. Sales taxes deal the hardest blow to those least able to afford them.
